About MusaiSearch

MusaiSearch is a privacy‑respecting, open metasearch experience powered by SearXNG. It aggregates results from many sources while avoiding tracking, advertising profiles, or storing your queries.

Learn more about the vision behind MusaiSearch on Find your muse.

Why use it?

  • No profiling: your searches are not logged or sold.
  • Open source: transparent code and community contributions.
  • Broad perspective: results are aggregated from multiple engines for balanced discovery.

Make it yours

Add this instance as your default search engine via OpenSearch, or run your own. MusaiSearch inherits the strengths of SearXNG while focusing on a calm, muse‑driven discovery experience.

Search syntax

MusaiSearch supports powerful query modifiers to guide your discovery journey. You can tweak categories, engines, languages, and more. See Preferences for available engines, categories, and languages.

! Select engine and category

Use a ! prefix to target specific engines or categories. Examples:

  • Search Wikipedia for paris: !wp paris or !wikipedia paris
  • Search in category map for paris: !map paris
  • Image search: !images Wau Holland

Abbreviations are accepted. Modifiers are chainable and inclusive, e.g. !map !ddg !wp paris.

: Select language

Use a : prefix to select a language filter. Example:

  • Search Wikipedia in French: :fr !wp Wau Holland

!!<bang> External bangs

MusaiSearch supports DuckDuckGo external bangs. Use !! to jump directly to an external search page, e.g. !!wfr Wau Holland.

Note: this opens an external site directly. MusaiSearch cannot protect your privacy once you leave this site.

!! automatic redirect

Include !! in your query (separated by spaces) to auto‑redirect to the first result, similar to “Feeling Lucky”. Example: !! Wau Holland.

Caution: redirect destinations can’t be verified for trustworthiness, and MusaiSearch cannot protect your privacy on external sites.

Special queries

Some utility queries (enable in Preferences):

  • Generate a random UUID: random uuid
  • Find the average: avg 123 548 2.04 24.2
  • Show your browser user agent: user-agent (if activated)
  • Hash digests: md5 lorem ipsum, sha512 lorem ipsum
